Privacy Policy — TrainMate

Effective Date: January 4, 2026 • Contact: AppTrainMate@gmail.com

Short version: TrainMate uses the preferences you enter to generate workouts and meal options. Most data is stored on your device. If you enable notifications, your device delivers reminders. We do not sell your personal information.

1) Overview

TrainMate is a fitness planning app that can generate workout plans, support a guided gym mode, show meal options (including ingredients and heating methods), and send workout reminders. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide

  • Fitness preferences (goal, skill level, focus areas, workout schedule/times, equipment preferences)
  • Diet preferences (allergens, meal filters)
  • Any text you enter in the app (for example, messages to an in-app coach if available)

B. Data stored on your device

  • Preferences and settings
  • Generated workout plans
  • Workout history/progress (streaks, completions)
  • Diet filters and viewed meal options

3) Permissions

Notifications: Used only to send workout reminders at the times you choose. You can disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings.

Camera/Photos (only if enabled): Used only if you choose to log meal photos or upload an image for features inside the app. You can deny access in iOS Settings.

4) Analytics and Crash Reporting

Choose the one that matches your app and delete the other paragraph:

Option A (No analytics): TrainMate does not use third-party analytics and does not collect usage data outside your device.

Option B (Uses analytics/crash tools): TrainMate may use analytics and crash reporting tools to improve stability (device model, OS version, crash logs, basic usage). We do not use this to identify you personally.

5) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only if you choose to share content (such as a streak card), if required by law, or with service providers strictly for analytics/crash reporting (if enabled).

6) Not Medical Advice

TrainMate provides general fitness and nutrition information for informational purposes only and is not medical advice. Consult a qualified professional for medical guidance.

7) Your Choices

  • Edit preferences inside the app
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored data

8) Children’s Privacy

TrainMate is not designed for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us at AppTrainMate@gmail.com.
