FAQ

I generated a plan but don’t see workouts.

  • Go back to the Survey and tap Generate Plan again.
  • Close and reopen the app.
  • If needed, delete and reinstall the app to clear local data.

Notifications aren’t working.

  • iPhone Settings → Notifications → TrainMate → Allow Notifications ON
  • Check Focus / Do Not Disturb
  • Verify your workout times in the app

Allergen search won’t add items when I tap.

  • Try tapping directly on the result row text.
  • Restart the app.
  • Update to the latest build.
