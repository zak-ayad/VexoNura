VexoNura – Support

Need help? Contact us at VexoNura@gmail.com.

Quick help

  • Workout reminders not showing? Check iOS Settings → Notifications → VexoNura and allow alerts.
  • Plans not updating? Try changing one survey option and generate again.
  • Diet/allergen filtering looks wrong? Make sure your allergens are selected correctly and regenerate the plan.
  • App crashes or bugs? Email us what happened and your iPhone model + iOS version.

Feature requests

Early Access means we’re improving fast. If you want a feature (new exercises, meal options, timers, progress tracking), email us what you’d like and why it helps.

Medical disclaimer

VexoNura provides general fitness and nutrition guidance and is not medical advice. If you have a medical condition, pain, or dizziness, consult a qualified professional.
